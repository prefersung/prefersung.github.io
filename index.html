Research Interests

My research focuses on improving recommender systems through generative modeling and cross-domain transfer:

  • Cross-Domain Recommendation — Transferring knowledge across domains to alleviate data sparsity
  • Cold-Start Problem — Addressing the challenge of recommending for new users/items with limited interactions
  • Generative Recommendation — Leveraging VAE, RQVAE, and seq2seq models for next-item prediction
  • Knowledge Distillation — Cross-domain knowledge distillation for domain adaptation in recommendation
  • Efficient LLM Deployment — Practical deployment of large models in real-world recommendation scenarios
